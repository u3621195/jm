Jab Moo rules

Exactly 4 players. Player holding 2♣ leads the first trick. Players must follow the led suit if possible; otherwise they may play anything. Highest card of the led suit wins the trick and leads next.

Important restrictions

First trick: hearts and Q♠ cannot be played.

Round scoring

♥2–10 = negative face value, J♥ = -20, Q♥ = -30, K♥ = -40, A♥ = -50. Q♠ = -100. J♦ = +100. 10♣ doubles the entire round score. All 13 hearts flips to +194. Grand shoot (all 13 hearts + Q♠ + J♦ + 10♣) scores +700 or +1,000 based on the pre-game setting.
